Red Dead Online
The Hunter's Calculator

When hunting it would be nice to know how much profit you were bringing home after
a long trip.

When the user makes a kill, they will press a button next to the animal icon of
their kill, and it will tally that profit of that animal to a total.

There will also be a running total of all animals in current hunting trip.

Functions:
  (Working) - User can press '-' or '+' to increment count of animals collected.
  (Working) - User can type in count of animals collected.
  Total estimated value will show up based on quality
  Running Total value will show up based on all animals collected in hunt.
